GenEraZions — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the GenEraZions mobile app ("GenEraZions", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the app. By using GenEraZions you agree to this policy.

Contact: mattyg585@icloud.com

1. Who we are

GenEraZions is a cross-generation pop-culture trivia game. The app is available on Google Play under the package com.mattyg585.generazions.

2. Information we collect

Account information you provide

  • Email address (used for sign-up and sign-in)
  • Username / in-game display name
  • Selected avatar (a generated illustration — we do not collect or store photos of you)

Gameplay information generated as you play

  • In-game virtual currency balances (coins) and points
  • Match history and results
  • Generation/era preference
  • Friend connections you create in the app

Technical & advertising information

  • A device advertising identifier, used by Google AdMob to serve ads
  • Standard technical data needed to deliver ads and keep the service running

Contacts (only if you choose to use the invite feature)

  • If you tap to invite friends, the app may read your device contacts on your device to let you pick people to invite. We do not upload or store your contact list on our servers.

We do not collect your precise location, and we do not collect photos, files, or media from your device.

3. How we use information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate sign-in
  • To run gameplay: matchmaking, hosted rooms, scoring, leaderboards, and virtual-coin balances
  • To display your username and avatar to other players during online matches
  • To show advertising via Google AdMob
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and operate and improve the service

We do not sell your personal information.

4. How information is shared

  • Supabase (our backend and database provider) stores your account and gameplay data on our behalf. Data is hosted in the United States.
  • Google AdMob receives your advertising identifier and ad-related signals to serve and measure ads. See Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob data disclosures.
  • Other players: your username, avatar, and in-match results are visible to other players in online and hosted-room matches.
  • Legal: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users' safety.

5. Data retention & deletion

We retain account and gameplay data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app (Settings → Delete Account). You may also request deletion by emailing mattyg585@icloud.com. Advertising identifiers are managed by your device; you can reset or limit them in your device settings.

6. Security

Data transmitted between the app and our backend is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Children's privacy

GenEraZions is intended for users age 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us information, contact us at mattyg585@icloud.com and we will delete it.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact mattyg585@icloud.com.

9. Advertising choices

GenEraZions shows ads through Google AdMob. You can opt out of personalized advertising and reset your advertising ID via your device settings (on Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, by notice in the app.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: mattyg585@icloud.com
