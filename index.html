IceCreamSocial Account & Data Deletion

IceCreamSocial gives users full control over their account and personal data. You may delete your account at any time directly within the app or by contacting us.

Delete Your Account in the App

  1. Open the IceCreamSocial app
  2. Navigate to your Profile or Settings
  3. Select Delete Account
  4. Confirm your request
Important: Once your account is deleted, it will be deactivated immediately. All associated personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 90 days.

Request Account or Data Deletion

If you are unable to access your account, you may request deletion of your account or personal data by contacting us at:

admin@icecreamsocial.com

Please include the email address associated with your account in your request. We will process your request in accordance with our data retention policies.

Data Retention

After an account deletion request is submitted, all user data is scheduled for deletion and permanently removed from our systems within 90 days, unless retention is required for legal or security purposes.
